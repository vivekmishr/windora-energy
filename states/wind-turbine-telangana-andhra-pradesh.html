Wind Turbine in Telangana & Andhra Pradesh

The Telangana–Andhra Pradesh region combines coastal AP's strong sea-breeze with Telangana's plateau winds — a productive market for vertical axis wind turbines. From Hyderabad villas to Visakhapatnam coast installations to remote farms, Windora serves the whole region.

Why Wind Energy in Telangana & Andhra Pradesh?

Telangana sits on the Deccan plateau (3-5 m/s), while coastal Andhra Pradesh enjoys exceptional Bay of Bengal sea-breeze (5-8 m/s). Combined, the region has diverse wind profiles suitable for our full product range.

Average annual wind: 3-8 m/s (best on AP coast)
Major distribution companies: TSSPDCL/TSNPDCL (Telangana), APSPDCL/APEPDCL/APCPDCL (AP)

Key factors driving wind adoption in Telangana & Andhra Pradesh:

  • Coastal AP — among India's best urban wind
  • Hyderabad IT/pharma sector ESG demand
  • AP cyclone exposure — handled by Windora 55 m/s rating
  • Strong farm/agriculture sector in AP — irrigation + cold storage demand
  • Telangana RE policy supportive of distributed renewable

State-Specific Considerations

Each Indian state has its own electricity tariff structure, net-metering rules, and renewable energy policy. In Telangana & Andhra Pradesh, the local DISCOM is TSSPDCL/TSNPDCL (Telangana), APSPDCL/APEPDCL/APCPDCL (AP), and we handle all the necessary documentation and applications as part of our turnkey installation service.

For accelerated payback, commercial buyers in Telangana & Andhra Pradesh should also explore:

  • Accelerated depreciation — 40-80% in year 1 under Income Tax Act
  • State-level renewable energy incentives — varies year to year, we check current schemes during quote
  • Net-metering credit — export excess generation at credit rates
  • NABARD / SFAC finance — for agricultural installations

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Telangana & Andhra Pradesh windy enough for a small wind turbine?

Yes — average winds in Telangana & Andhra Pradesh are within Windora's productive range (we start at 3.5 m/s). We confirm specific site wind via a free survey before quoting.

Typically 4-8 weeks from signed quote to live generation. Includes civil works, electrical installation, and DISCOM net-metering paperwork.
