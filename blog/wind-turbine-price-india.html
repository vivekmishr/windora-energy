What Affects the Price

1. Tower height

Higher towers reach better wind. A 3 kW turbine on a 6m tower costs ~10% less than the same turbine on a 9m tower — but generates ~25% less. The math usually favours taller towers.

2. Foundation type

Rooftop installations are cheaper (smaller foundation). Ground installations need bigger concrete foundations, especially in soft soil.

3. Location

Remote sites (hills, islands, border areas) add logistics premium. Cities along the Mumbai-Delhi corridor are cheapest. Coastal installations add marine-grade coatings (~5-10% premium).

4. Site complexity

Distance from main meter, cable run length, scaffolding needs, society/permits complexity — all add to price.

5. Battery option

Lithium battery bank (LiFePO4) adds ₹40,000-60,000 per kWh of storage. A 10 kWh bank adds ~₹4-6 lakh.

6. Inverter type

Hybrid inverters with full grid + battery + generator handling cost more than simple grid-tie inverters. We size based on your needs.

Hidden Costs to Watch (Other Brands)

  • "Turbine-only" quotes: Some vendors quote just the turbine, then add ₹50,000-1.5 lakh for tower, foundation, and installation. Always insist on an installed price.
  • Excluded electrical work: Some quotes exclude cables, MCBs, and net-metering — adds ₹20,000-50,000.
  • Civil works charges: Foundation digging, RCC, mounting plates can be billed separately.
  • Transportation & loading: Some vendors charge ex-works prices, adding ₹15,000-50,000 for transport.
  • "Onsite engineer" fees: Charged per day for cheap installer crews.

How to Compare Quotes Properly

When comparing wind turbine quotes from multiple vendors, normalise on these points:

  1. Is the price fully installed (turn-key)? Same scope across vendors?
  2. What's the tower height? Same height for apples-to-apples?
  3. What's the warranty period and what does it cover?
  4. Is the expected annual generation stated in writing?
  5. Does the price include net-metering application?
  6. Is the first-year service free? What's the AMC cost from year 2?
  7. Is the vendor a manufacturer or trader? (Manufacturers usually offer better long-term support.)

Government Subsidies & Tax Benefits

For commercial buyers, India's Income Tax Act allows 40-80% accelerated depreciation on renewable energy assets in the first year — a major tax saving. Agricultural and rural installations may qualify for NABARD / state-level renewable schemes. We help identify applicable benefits at the quote stage.
