Frequently Asked Questions

FASTag is an RFID-based electronic toll collection sticker affixed to your vehicle's windshield. As you pass a toll plaza, an antenna scans your tag and the toll amount is automatically deducted from your linked wallet — no cash, no stopping. It's mandatory across all national highways and accepted at NHAI toll plazas pan-India.
You will need: (1) Vehicle Registration Certificate (RC), (2) KYC ID — Aadhaar / PAN / Passport / Voter ID, (3) Address proof, (4) Passport-size photograph of the owner. Commercial vehicles may require additional fleet/operator documents.
Activation is instant — most FASTags issued by RVDS are usable within minutes of purchase. Once your KYC and vehicle RC are validated, your tag links to your wallet and is ready for toll deduction immediately.
Yes. Per NHAI/MoRTH directive, FASTag is mandatory for all four-wheelers and above using national highways. Vehicles without FASTag are charged double the toll fee in cash lanes. Two-wheelers are currently exempt.
