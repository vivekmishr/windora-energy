What's Included in the MRP

  1. Wind Turbine — rotor, blades, generator (PMSG)
  2. Controller — charge / grid controller appropriate to configuration
  3. Inverter — sized to match the rated output
  4. Hardware accessories — mounting brackets, fasteners, basic cabling, junction boxes

Additional Costs (NOT in MRP)

  1. DISCOM commissioning charges — net-metering application fees, charged by your local electricity board (MSEDCL / BESCOM / TANGEDCO / etc.)
  2. Transportation — varies by distance from our Mira Road, Maharashtra warehouse
  3. Fabrication & Installation — civil foundation, tower assembly, electrical installation; cost depends on site complexity
  4. GST @ 5% — applicable on the MRP and installation charges
  5. Battery storage — optional, ₹40,000-60,000 per kWh of lithium LiFePO4 capacity
  6. Tower height upgrade — standard height included; taller mast extra

How to Read the Price List

  1. kW (kilowatt) rating is the turbine's rated power, not its average output. Real-world generation is shown in the Daily Generation column.
  2. Type E turbines start at lower wind speeds (~1.5 m/s) but produce less peak power — ideal for low-wind sites in cities.
  3. Type H turbines are higher efficiency at moderate winds — ideal for farmhouses, open sites, and most commercial installations.
  4. Phase 1 models suit residential single-phase connections. Phase 3 models are for commercial/industrial three-phase supply.
  5. Hybrid configuration (available 3.5 kW+) lets the turbine work alongside solar PV + battery for 24×7 generation.

FAQ — Pricing & Discounts

Are the prices fixed or negotiable?

Listed prices are MRP (Maximum Retail Price). Discounts are available based on project size, payment terms, location, and current promotional offers. Request a quote to receive your discounted price.

What's the total installed cost vs MRP?

Typical installed cost is MRP + 25-40% to cover transportation, civil foundation, tower fabrication, electrical work, commissioning, and DISCOM paperwork. Example: a 5 kW H turbine at ₹7.38 lakh MRP usually installs at ₹9.5-10.5 lakh complete. Exact figure depends on site distance and complexity.

Do you offer EMI / financing?

Yes — partnered with select banks and NBFCs for residential and commercial financing. Typical terms: 5-7 year tenure, 10-12% interest. For agriculture, NABARD-linked concessional rates may apply. Discuss during quote.

Are there government subsidies?

Subsidies vary by state and year. Gujarat, Maharashtra, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, and Rajasthan currently have the most supportive small-wind schemes. Commercial buyers qualify for 40-80% accelerated depreciation in year one under Income Tax Act section 32 — a major effective price reduction.

What warranty is included?

15-25 year warranty on the turbine and tower (depending on model). 10-25 year on inverter. 5-10 year on batteries (if applicable). First-year service free. Extended AMC available from year 2.

How long until I get my quote?

For pricing only: within 4-6 hours via WhatsApp/email. For a full design + binding quote (after site survey): 3-5 days. Free site visit anywhere in India.

Can I order online?

Currently we work on a quote-to-order basis since every site needs proper wind assessment + sizing. Submit your details and we'll send a written quote you can accept directly — no haggling, no surprises.
