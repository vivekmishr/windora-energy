Why Choose Vertical Axis Wind Turbines?

At Windora Energy, we believe in a future powered by clean, renewable energy. Our cutting-edge vertical axis wind turbines (VAWTs) represent the next generation of wind power technology — designed to work efficiently in urban environments, residential rooftops, and commercial installations.

Unlike traditional horizontal turbines, our VAWTs capture wind from any direction, operate silently, and integrate seamlessly into modern architecture.

Our Technology

Advanced VAWT Engineering

Decades of innovation packed into every turbine

Darrieus Vertical Axis Design

Our Darrieus-style turbines use aerodynamic lift principles similar to aircraft wings. Perfect for high-wind residential and commercial applications.

  • Power output: 1-10 kW
  • Start-up wind: 2.5 m/s
  • Rated wind: 11 m/s
  • Efficiency: up to 40%

Savonius Drag-Based Design

Drag-based turbines that excel in low-wind conditions. Ideal for urban environments and locations with variable wind patterns.

  • Power output: 0.5-5 kW
  • Start-up wind: 1.5 m/s
  • Self-starting capability
  • Works in turbulent winds

Hybrid Darrieus-Savonius

The best of both worlds — combining lift and drag principles for maximum efficiency across all wind conditions. Our flagship technology.

  • Power output: 2-20 kW
  • Start-up wind: 1.8 m/s
  • Efficiency: up to 45%
  • Self-starting + high performance
