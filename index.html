About Windora

R&D-Driven Wind Energy, Built in India

Windora Energy is focused on a simple mission: make wind power practical for Indian homes, farms, and businesses — not just massive wind farms in Tamil Nadu.

We design vertical axis wind turbines specifically optimized for India's unique wind profile: low average speeds, turbulent gusts, rooftops, and mixed solar-wind conditions. Every product is iteratively tested and tuned for the environments our customers actually live and work in.

From residential rooftops in Mumbai to irrigation pumps in Rajasthan, our turbines are helping Indians generate their own clean, reliable power — and push back against rising grid tariffs and unpredictable supply.
