Wind Turbine in Lucknow

Lucknow's growing residential market, frequent power cuts, and high summer AC loads make wind energy increasingly attractive. From Gomti Nagar bungalows to Hazratganj offices to farmhouses on the city periphery, Windora delivers vertical axis turbines across the UP capital region.

Why Wind Energy Works in Lucknow

Lucknow sits on the open Gangetic plains with moderate, consistent winds. Annual averages range from 3-5 m/s, with strong pre-monsoon and monsoon winds blowing through Uttar Pradesh. That makes Lucknow a good fit for Windora's vertical axis wind turbines, which start generating at just 3.5 m/s.

  • UPPCL tariffs rising with consumption-slab structure
  • Frequent rural power cuts (still common in surrounding UP)
  • High AC load from May through September
  • Growing tech and government sector workforce
  • Many farmhouses in periphery — Mohanlalganj, Malihabad, Barabanki

Real example: A 3 kW Helical on a Gomti Nagar bungalow generates ~5,000 kWh/year. Saves ~₹45,000/year. Payback 5-6 years.

FAQ — Wind Turbine Lucknow

Is the UP capital windy enough?

Yes — 3-5 m/s average is well within our turbine's operating range (we start at 3.5 m/s). Pre-monsoon winds are especially strong in central UP.

Can it power my Mohanlalganj farmhouse?

Yes — periphery farmhouses often have excellent wind exposure. Off-grid hybrid systems are popular here for full energy independence.

How fast is UPPCL net-metering approval?

Typically 4-8 weeks. We handle the full application paperwork through UPPCL/MVVNL.
