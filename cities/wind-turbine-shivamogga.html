FAQ — Wind Turbine Shivamogga

Is Shivamogga windy enough for a small wind turbine?

Shivamogga (Shimoga), in the Malnad foothills, sees reliable monsoon and seasonal winds. Our vertical axis turbines start generating at just 3.5 m/s, so they perform reliably across Shivamogga and Shivamogga district.

Which DISCOM handles net-metering in Shivamogga?

MESCOM manages the local grid connection in Shivamogga. We handle the entire net-metering application and paperwork as part of our turnkey installation.

Should I choose wind, solar, or hybrid in Shivamogga?

For most Karnataka sites we recommend a hybrid solar+wind system — solar covers sunny afternoons while wind covers evenings, nights, and the monsoon, giving the most consistent year-round output.

Can you install off-grid for a remote Shivamogga farm?

Yes. Given the strong 4-6 m/s wind resource around Shivamogga, off-grid and battery-backed hybrid systems work very well for remote farms and farmhouses with weak or no grid supply.
