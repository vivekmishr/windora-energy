Why Rooftop Wind Turbines Make Sense in Indian Cities

Indian cities have millions of unused rooftops collecting nothing but heat and rainwater. The same rooftop that fits a few solar panels can also host a vertical axis rooftop wind turbine — combining solar by day and wind by night and monsoon. Rooftop wind generates extra power exactly when solar can't.

Horizontal turbines don't work on rooftops. They need open undisturbed wind and lots of clearance. Vertical axis turbines (VAWT) thrive in the turbulent, swirling rooftop winds that destroy horizontal-axis performance. That's why every rooftop wind turbine installed in urban India is vertical.

Rooftop Wind + Solar = Best of Both

Most rooftops have plenty of room for both. The wind turbine sits on a small pole (often above the parapet) while solar panels cover the flat surface. The two sources complement each other perfectly, and our hybrid systems combine them with a single controller and inverter — one integrated install, one warranty, lower cost than installing separately.

Rooftop Installation Considerations

  1. Roof load capacity — concrete RCC roofs almost always work; we verify with a structural visit
  2. Society / association approval — required for apartments; we provide all documentation
  3. Local building bye-laws — small wind turbines (≤10 kW) are exempt from most state permits
  4. Anti-vibration mounts — mandatory on all our rooftop installations
  5. Lightning protection — included; turbine itself acts as part of building's LP system
  6. Earthing — connect to building's existing earth pit

FAQ

Will rooftop wind turbines damage my building?

No. We use vibration-isolated mounts that decouple the turbine from your building structure. Years of installations across Indian cities show zero impact on roof integrity when installed by certified professionals.

Do I need building permits in India?

For most ≤10 kW small wind turbines on private property, no permits are required. Apartment societies need internal approval. We handle all documentation.

Will my neighbours hear the turbine?

Operating noise is below 35 dB at 5m — quieter than ambient city noise. Most neighbours don't even notice. We can do a noise demo at our existing rooftop installation in Mumbai.
